Introduction

The research of operations understood as the complex mathematical the discipline which is engaged in construction, working out and application mathematical models of acceptance of optimum decisions, is one from most intensively developing sections of applied mathematics also has numerous appendices.

Origin of research of operations as scientific disciplines and occurrence of the term "research of operations" concerns the Second World War beginning, and have been caused practical necessity of the best organisation of the large-scale actions, and also forecasting of their outcome at acceptance command of various decisions.

The further development of research of operations is closely connected with scientific and technical revolution which is accompanied by the rough development and sharp complication of technics; with substantial growth scale of the actions spent in various spheres of the human activity; with disproportionate increase of expenses material and time resources on their realisation; with wide introduction the computing technicians and mathematical methods in management sphere.

In present time of research of operations find wide application in the decision of the most different practical problems, beginning from forward planning of the scientific workings out and finishing forecasting of development of sphere of service. It is connected by that any operation, including military, represents set of purposeful actions, and research of operations - search of ways of achievement one or several the purposes.
