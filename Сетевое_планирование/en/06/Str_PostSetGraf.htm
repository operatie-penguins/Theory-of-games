Construction network the column. A critical way

At the organization of works above various projects, planning of complexes of works the wide circulation was received with methods of network planning and management.

the Initial information on the project is set by the list of works, their duration, sequence of performance (for each work should be specified, it precedes what works or what follows). The network schedule of the project name evident representation of the project in the form of a network. Arches of this network represent operations (work) and are focused; the direction of arches corresponds to process of realization of the program in time. The attitude of ordering between operations is set by means of events. Event is defined as the moment of time when one operations come to the end both others begin, and it is represented in a network in unit (top).

Criteria with which should satisfy the network schedule:

1) any work presented by an arch, leaving some top, cannot begin before end of all works presented by arches, entering in the given top;

2) each pair events should connect no more than one work;

3) in the network schedule should be initial and final event.

For performance of the listed requirements introduction of fictitious works (the works of zero duration which are not demanding an expense of any resources) is admissible.

The Critical way defines continuous sequence of the critical works connecting initial and finishing events of a network.
