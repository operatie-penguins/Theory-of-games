Graphic way of construction of a critical way

At a graphic way of definition of a critical way it is necessary to consider criteria with which should satisfy the network schedule:

1) any work presented by an arch, leaving some top, cannot begin before end of all works presented by arches, entering in the given top;

2) each pair events should connect no more than one work;

3) in the network schedule should be initial and final event.

For performance of the listed requirements introduction of fictitious works (the works of zero duration which are not demanding an expense of any resources) is admissible.
